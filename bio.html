~ Biography ~

Caroline McBrien grew up on Long Island, New York with her two younger sisters, and later, the cutest black lab in the world, Tucker. When she was younger, she spent her days reading, dancing ballet, and traveling around Canada to visit family.

Caroline now lives in Boston and goes to school at Tufts University, where she studies Computer Science. When she is not furiously coding in Halligan (the Tufts CS building), she can be found at the WMFO radio station, where she has her own radio show. She is also one of the co-presidents of the Mock Trial team at Tufts, and devotes almost all of her time to making sure the program is running smoothly. Her hobbies now include hiking, collecting postcards, and drinking tea.
